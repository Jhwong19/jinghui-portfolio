We Build, Ship, and Learn.

↳ /BUILD

Born in code, raised by real systems. I build with LLMs, data pipelines, and the unglamorous plumbing in between — the kind of work that survives Monday morning. Every model I deploy answers to a real user, a real cost line, and a real outcome.

↳ /SHIP

Shipping beats speculating. I prefer a rough v1 in production over a polished deck, because contact with reality is the only honest feedback loop. Iterate in public, measure what changed, and keep the cadence — momentum compounds.

↳ /LEARN

Every shipped system is a question waiting for data to answer it. I treat metrics, user behaviour, and failure modes as curriculum, not noise. The half-life of any AI stack is short, so the real moat is the speed at which you absorb what worked, what didn’t, and why.
